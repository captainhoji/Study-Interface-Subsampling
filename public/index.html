Making Line Charts More Accessible to Blind People

This experiment is part of a research project on making line charts more accessible to blind people.
You will listen to some numeric data, and draw a line chart on the computer.
Do not use external resources.

IMPORTANT: The quality of your answer will impact the reward.

This experiment involves sound, so you need speakers or headphones to participate.

Note: Internet Explorer is NOT supported. Chrome is recommended. Otherwise you will not be able to complete this experiment.
